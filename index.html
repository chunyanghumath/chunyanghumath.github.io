My research interests lie in discrete geometric analysis, especially discrete curvatures on connection graphs and eigenvalue estimates for Laplacian on graphs.

I have applied for "Outstanding PhD Students Overseas Study Program of the University of Science and Technology of China" to visit Professor Norbert Peyerimhoff at Duhram University in the U.K. for a whole year, from May 1st, 2025 to April 30th, 2026.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Central China Normal University, Wuhan, China.
